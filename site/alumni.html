KUCSE : Alumni Directory

KUCSE Alumni Directory  is an initiative aimed at bridging the gap between our esteemed alumni and current students, making it easier for students to seek guidance and inspiration while offering alumni a meaningful way to give back to the department.

We are deeply grateful to the alumni who have taken the time to graciously share their profiles, and we hope the insights and experiences shared by our alumni will help the students navigate their own future journeys - no matter the path they choose.
